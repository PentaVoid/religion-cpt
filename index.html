Enjoy the wonders of Buddhism in Russia. Although it only makes up 1% of the population, it makes up 100% of the fun. Explore religious sites, learn about Buddhist history, rituals, and many more!

History

Buddhism appeared in Russia in early 17th century, when Kalmyk tribes following the Gelug school of Tibetan Buddhism became Russian citizens. However this was only one of some ways Buddhism arose in Russia. In Buryatia, a Russian republic, the neighbouring Mongolian Buddhists influenced the republic to such a degree that it became the Buddhist capital of Russia. In its early stages, Buddhists would gather at prayer tents to pray, but as Buddhism developed, permanent monasteries were built for prayer.
