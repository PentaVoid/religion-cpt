Buddhistdoor Global

"BDG is expressly non-sectarian and strives to promote an egalitarian, progressive vision of the Buddhadharma that embraces all Buddhist traditions. In line with our aspiration to offer a free and open medium for sharing the riches of this ancient spiritual tradition—through which individuals and communities can learn, connect, and interact—our content is, and always will be, freely available to all."



Connection to Faith

Although Orginally Based In China, Buddhistdoor.Net Has Expanded To Various Parts Of Russia, With Many Of Its Writers Based There. By Raising Awareness Through Online Blogs And Articles About The Many Parts Of Buddhism Around The World, This Organization Allows Russian Buddhists To Not Only Learn About Buddism In Russia, But Wordwide. This Allows For A More Full Understanding Of Buddhism For All Buddhists. Not Only That, It Brings Up Many Important Buddhist Related Events, Such As Festivals, Teachings, And Others. By Raising Awareness On These, One Can Enrich Their Buddhist Experience By Not Only Learning, But Enjoying The Many Things To Do With The Buddhist Faith.





























Get Involved!


Although You May Not Be Writing One Of The Many Articles, There Are Many Other Ways To Get Involved. You Can Comment On These Articles, Along With Thousands Of Other Buddhists, To Share Opinions And Connect With Communities Around The World. It Creates An Oppurtunity To Find New Friends, And Learn From One Another. Furthermore, You Can Also Donate To This NPO, To Help Support The Authors And Continue To Keep The Site Free For All To Use.
